```json
  "title": "AI Governance Summit",
  "keywords": [
    {"category": "AI Governance & Ethics", "keyword": "Responsible AI"},
    {"category": "Regulatory & Legal Frameworks", "keyword": "AI Policy"},
    {"category": "Social Impact & Inclusion", "keyword": "Digital Inclusion"}
  ],
  ...
}
```

- Quiz/LinkedIn can match on **category** (broad match) or **keyword** (exact match)
- Scoring: Category match = 5 points, Keyword match = 10 points
- Easier to maintain than 1,624 unique values
